Invitation

It gives me immense pleasure to invite you in the Campus Recruitment Program 2013 of IET Lucknow to recruit its final year students for placement in your esteemed organization. This website describes the academic programs offered at IET Lucknow and about the campus life at IET which highlights the excellence in academics apart from various extra and co-curricular activities designed for total personality development of the students.
